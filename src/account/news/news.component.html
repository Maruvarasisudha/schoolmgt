An exam is for self-improvement. An exam is a formal test that you take to show your knowledge or ability in a particular subject, or to obtain a qualification. The word exam is short for examination, from 19th century student slang. An exam usually takes place in a class and consists of questions that test a student's knowledge. Sometimes the word describes the of medical test of some kind, like an eye exam, a dental exam, or a physical exam.
